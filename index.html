Dark is a German sci-fi thriller released as a series on Netflix, co-created by Baran bo Odar and Jantje Friese.

Plot:

In the aftermath of a child's disappearance in Winden, connections between four estranged families start to unravel a sinister time travel conspiracy which spans several generations.

The series explores the existential implications of time, and its effect on human nature.
